Introduction

Welcome to FeedGrain Visual System! The system enables you to visualize the US historical feed grain data and gain insights into supply and demand of feed grains in domestic market and international trade of the US with import and export partners. The system even can help you briefly review history after the American Civil War.

Feed grain (corn, sorghum, barley, oats) is any grain grown for feeding livestock. As a component of food chain, their sufficient and reliable supplies help stabilize food supplies and thereby ensure food security, and even contribute to national security. Also, feed grain is an important agricultural commodity in global market which plays an essential role in determining ranking and influence of a nation across the globe.

The US is a major producer of feed grains in the world, whose exports of feed grains in all forms in 2015-16 amount to 3.96 billion bushels. However, production and consumption vary across grains. The visual system will render the whole story of the data for you.
